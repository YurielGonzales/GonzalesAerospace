Aerospace engineering is the primary field of engineering concerned
with the design, development, testing, and production of aircraft, spacecraft, and
related systems and equipment. The field has traditionally focused on problems
related to atmospheric and space flight, with two major and overlapping
branches: aeronautical engineering and astronautical engineering.
